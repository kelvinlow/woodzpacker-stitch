The Genesis | 缘起

Heart of the Jungle

Agarwood is not merely wood; it is the miraculous result of a tree's healing process. For over a century, the Aquilaria tree secreted rare resins to protect itself from the elements, concentrating its essence into what we now know as Kinam. Each bead in this mala has been hand-carved to preserve the natural grain and resinous veins.

在越南深山之中，沉香树历经百年风霜与自然侵蚀，在自我修护中凝聚出稀世油脂。奇楠，是沉香中的极品，其形成极具偶然性，千百棵树中难求其一。
