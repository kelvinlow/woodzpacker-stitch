The Art of
Carving & Polish

Every bead in a Scholar’s Atelier strand is individually selected for grain direction and density. Our masters use traditional dry-grinding techniques that preserve the natural oils within the wood.

We do not use waxes or synthetic polishes. Instead, the wood is buffed with raw silk until it achieves a soft, internal glow—a reflection of the inner peace these objects are meant to facilitate.
